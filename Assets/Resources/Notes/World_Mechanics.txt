World mechanics:
	
	Starts off with 3 different worlds
		-Forest World
		-Desert World
		-Castle World
	Forest world is starting area
	Desert world unlocks after a certain level
	Castle World unlocks after certain level
	
	As you level up so worlds
	Leveled up worlds have stronger enmies better drops and adds monsters
	
	Random Events can happen while in a world
	
	Once world reaches max level you can fight its boss
	
	More worlds will be added on later
